Dhabeli Recipe

Description

It is an Indian variation of Burger, with Spiced Potato filling along with tomato sauce and chutney with bhujia garnishing sandwiched between two sides of one pav.

Ingredients

  • Pav
  • Potatoes
  • Spices
  • Bhujia
  • Sauce and Chutney

Steps to Prepare

  1. Gather all ingredients for dabeli stuffing.
  2. Add spices to mashed boiled potatoes.
  3. Spread the potato mixture in a tray.
  4. Add sev, peanuts, pomegranate seeds, and cilantro.
  5. Apply chutneys to the dinner rolls.
  6. Apply the stuffing.
